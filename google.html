Who was the founder of Google?

The founders of Google are Larry Page and Sergey Brin. They both studied at Stanford University as PhD students and met there in 1995. In 1996, they built together a search engine (initially called BackRub) which had links that could find the importance of individual webpages.

How did they invent Google?

The purpose of Google is to collect and manage the world's information so that people around the world can access the information and make use of it. Larry and Sergey built Google in a dorm room at Stanford University. Google Inc. was introduced in 1998, when Sun co-founder of Google, Any Bectolsheim wrote them a check ($100,000) to that entity. They named it Google as they were playing with the word "googol", which is the mathematical term for the digit 1 followed by 100 zeros.

How does Google make money?

Google generates its income mostly from advertising. Businesses use Google's AdWords Program to promote their products and services with targeted advertising which would attract more consumers. Google also has "third-parties" that make up their Google network uses their Google AdSense program to deliver appropriate ads that make income and improve the user experience.

What does Google do for us?

Google searches information from all over the world for people, so that they can find any information they want from around world. Google has several of products (e.g. Gmail, Youtube, Google+ etc.) that help people share their thoughts, videos and messages around. Google’s goal is to make everything (including their products) faster and better so that people can get things done faster and then spend time on the things they enjoy to do.

What does Google do for businesses?

Google provides a variety of tools and programs to help businesses succeed on and off the web. These programs are the strength of the business which enables entrepreneurs and publishers to develop their products/programs. Google’s advertising programs help other businesses to find customers/consumers. It also helps publishers make money off of their content. Google also provides cloud computing tools for other businesses which help the businesses save money and be more productive.

What does Google do for the web?

Google builds products which they are hoping to make the web better, which would allow people’s experience on the web better. Google wants to make Chrome and Android simpler and faster for people to do what they want to online. Google is also committed to open the web, so they’re involved in many different types of projects that help make it easier for developers to contribute to the online ecosystem and improve the web for the future. The web has developed much since Google was invented, but one thing that hasn’t changed is their belief in the endless possibilities of the Internet itself.
