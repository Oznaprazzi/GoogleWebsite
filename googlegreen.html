A better web.Better for the environment.

Google is improving their web so that it is better for the environment. They are making their company environmentally friendly by using resources efficiently and supporting renewable power. This means that when we use Google products, we’re making the environment better.

Renewable energy


Google is trying to power their company with 100% renewable energy. In addition to the environmental benefits, renewable energy is seen as a business opportunity for Google and continue to invest in building its development. Google believes that by using renewable energy to power the web, they’re creating a better and more improved future for everyone.

Using green power at Google

Google is currently using renewable energy to power over 30% of their operations, and they are continuing to look for other ways to increase their use of clean energy. This includes trying new, creative technology at their offices and buying green power near their data centres.

Investing in a clean energy future

Google is investing in clean energy do it’s more accessible for their company and for us as well. They’ve committed over $1 billion to renewable energy products, which helps to form a clean energy future for their business and the environment.

Google is recognized for our green efforts

Google has been recognized for their obligation to using and investing renewable power, and for decreasing their overall environmental impact. Google is very proud of these achievements and are excited to support collaboration across the industry. Google co-sounded the Climate Savers Computing Initiative in 2007 and joined The Green Grid – two global groups dedicated to greater and more improved efficiency and sustainability standards.
